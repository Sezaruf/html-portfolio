The Best Movies According To Cesar

My top 3 movies of all-time

Society Of Snow

One of my favorite movies. A plane crashes at 11.700 feet in the Andes on October 13 of 1972. Show the sacrifice of this survivors and what they had to do in order to survive.

The Godfather 2

I love mob movies, this one in particular shows the past of Don Vito Corleone and I really loved De Niro and Al Pacino's acting skills.

Deadpool And Wolverine

It might not be the greatest piece of art when it comes to cinematography, but it is a really funny movie and a collab that a lot of people (me included) has been waiting for.
